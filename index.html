About Me

My name is Tony and I am a second year student studying Computer Engineering at University of Waterloo. Since I was a young child, I've always been astonished by the amazing things that technology accomplish. I was especially passionate about computers and video games. As a child, I always dreamed to be a software developer and being able to create my own games. Life is full of challenges and so is programming. I really like to tackle a problem as hard as I can and enjoy the sense of accomplishment when I solve it. The first programming language I learned was Java. From there, I taught myself C#, Javascript, jQuery, Android Development and more.

In my spare time, I like to play sports. I really enjoy playing basketball, table tennis and soccer. I also love watching movies with my friends. In addition to sports, I also have a passion for music. I joined many bands at school and I play the French horn and piano.
